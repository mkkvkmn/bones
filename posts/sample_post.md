# Welcome to Bones

Welcome to my first post using **Bones**, the minimalist static site generator!

## Why Bones?

Bones is lightweight, easy to use, and efficient. It's perfect for those who need a straightforward static site generator without the extra complexity.

### Features

- **Fast and Simple**: Convert your Markdown files to HTML in no time.
- **Minimalist Design**: Focus on your content without worrying about unnecessary features.
- **Easy to Extend**: Add more features as you grow your site.
